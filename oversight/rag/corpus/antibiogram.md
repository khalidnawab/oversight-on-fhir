# Institutional Antibiogram (synthetic, 2026)

## Staphylococcus aureus (MSSA)
MSSA isolates are susceptible to cefazolin and oxacillin. Cefazolin is the preferred
narrow-spectrum agent for MSSA bacteremia once methicillin susceptibility is confirmed.
Vancomycin is not preferred for MSSA when a beta-lactam is tolerated.

## Escherichia coli
Local E. coli susceptibility to ceftriaxone is 88%. Ceftriaxone is an appropriate narrowing
target for ceftriaxone-susceptible E. coli bloodstream infection.

## Pseudomonas aeruginosa
Empiric antipseudomonal coverage (cefepime, piperacillin-tazobactam) should not be narrowed
unless an antipseudomonal susceptibility result is available.
