# Institutional Formulary Notes (synthetic)

## Cefazolin
Preferred narrow-spectrum cephalosporin for MSSA. Standard dose 1-2 g IV q8h; adjust for renal
function. First-line for de-escalation from piperacillin-tazobactam when MSSA is confirmed and
no beta-lactam allergy is documented.

## Ceftriaxone
Once-daily dosing; preferred for susceptible Enterobacterales. Avoid co-administration with
IV calcium-containing solutions.

## De-escalation policy
De-escalate empiric broad-spectrum therapy to the narrowest effective agent once culture and
susceptibility results are available, absent contraindications. Document rationale.
